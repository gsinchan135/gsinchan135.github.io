Influences

Something I am interested in is machine learning. I find machine learning extremely fascinating primarily because if it is capable of being applied to countless other fields in and out of computer science. Machine learning specifically interests me because of the benefits that it can have for humanity. Medical imaging can help analyze and process images with greater accuracy.

A person that inspires and influences me is Michael Reeves. He's very popular for his comedy videos which showcase his crazy inventions and robots. Michael takes a science that is generally considered difficult or boring and turns into extremely interesting and educational videos even to someone who may not understand computer science or engineering. I enjoy his videos because they show how fun computer science can be.
